Terms of Service

Welcome to Luxe Dine. These terms and conditions outline the rules and regulations for the use of our In-House QR Ordering System.

1. In-House Dining Only

Our digital ordering system is strictly for customers dining physically inside the restaurant. Orders placed through this system will be served to the specific table associated with the QR code scanned.

2. Order Acceptance

Once an order is placed through the digital menu and payment is confirmed, the order is sent directly to our kitchen. We reserve the right to refuse service or cancel orders in case of unforeseen circumstances.

3. Pricing and Payments

All prices listed on the digital menu are inclusive of applicable taxes unless stated otherwise. Payments are processed securely via our payment partner, Razorpay.

4. User Conduct

Users must not misuse the QR ordering system or attempt to disrupt its functionality. Any fraudulent activity will be reported to the authorities.

5. Limitation of Liability

Luxe Dine shall not be held responsible for any technical delays or errors in the ordering process, although we will make every effort to resolve any issues promptly.
