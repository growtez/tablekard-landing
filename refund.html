Refund & Cancellation

Our commitment is to provide fresh and high-quality meals. Please review our policy regarding order changes and refunds.

1. Cancellation Policy

Since this is an in-house dining system designed for immediate preparation, orders cannot be cancelled once they have been accepted by our kitchen. We recommend reviewing your cart carefully before making payment.

2. Order Errors

If there is an error in your order (e.g., wrong item served), please notify your table server immediately. We will rectify the error by providing the correct item or issuing a credit/refund as appropriate.

3. Refund Processing

In cases where a refund is approved by management, it will be processed through Razorpay back to the original payment method. Generally, refunds take 5-7 business days to reflect in your account.

4. Quality Issues

If you are unsatisfied with the quality of a dish, please speak to our restaurant manager. Refunds for quality issues are at the sole discretion of the management.
