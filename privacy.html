Privacy Policy

At Luxe Dine, we value your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data when you use our QR ordering system.

1. Information Collection

We collect minimal information necessary to process your order, which may include your phone number, table number, and payment details (processed by Razorpay).

2. Use of Information

Your data is used solely for processing your order, facilitating payment, and providing you with a digital receipt. We do not sell or share your personal data with third-party marketers.

3. Secure Payments

All financial transactions are handled by Razorpay, a secure and PCI-compliant payment gateway. Luxe Dine does not store your credit card or bank details on our servers.

4. Data Protection

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access or disclosure.

5. Cookies

Our digital menu may use essential cookies to maintain your session and order cart while you browse.
