Privacy Policy

At Tablekard, we respect your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information collected through our QR ordering system for restaurants.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the minimum information needed to process orders and support restaurants. This may include a table identifier, order details, and contact information provided during checkout.

2. How We Use Information

Data is used to fulfill orders, provide receipts, and support restaurant operations. We do not sell personal information to third-party marketers.

3. Payments

Payments are processed by secure payment partners. Tablekard does not store full card details on our servers.

4. Data Protection

We use industry-standard safeguards to protect data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

5. Cookies

Our digital menus may use essential cookies to keep sessions active and maintain order carts.
